Wide Angle Agile

What is Wide Angle Agile?

Wide Angle Agile is a practical, compliance‑ready approach to agile delivery for regulated environments. It expands the traditional agile lens to include risk, documentation, verification, and user experience without slowing teams down.

Why It Works

  • Integrates compliance and agility without unnecessary overhead
  • Supports clear, audit‑ready documentation as part of normal work
  • Improves cross‑functional alignment and decision‑making
  • Strengthens risk visibility and mitigation throughout development

Core Principles

  • Clarity over volume in documentation
  • Risk‑informed decision‑making at every stage
  • Human‑centered communication and collaboration
  • Iterative learning that includes compliance, not avoids it

Who It’s For

Wide Angle Agile is designed for teams working in regulated industries—medical devices, healthcare, finance, and any environment where safety, quality, and traceability matter.
